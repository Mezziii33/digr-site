The Problem

Music discovery is completely broken for working DJs.

Thousands of tracks release every week across Beatport, Bandcamp, Juno Download, and beyond. No tool helps you find what actually sounds like what you're looking for — you're left digging manually through endless releases, hoping something clicks.

01

Days — sometimes weeks

A single set can require 2–5 days of research, sometimes stretching into one or two weeks. And from 1,000 tracks reviewed, you might walk away with 50 or 100 worth keeping. The search-to-find ratio is brutal.

02

Key and BPM only goes so far

Existing tools let you filter by key or BPM — but that's where it stops. There's no way to search by groove, texture, energy, or sonic feel. The filters scratch the surface and leave the hard work to you.

03

Previewing blindly is exhausting

Every promising track means jumping from one platform to another just to hear 90 seconds. Hours gone — hours that could've been spent sharpening your mixing skills or actually building the set.
